## Introduction

Welcome to Object2Workout, an **AI-powered Bible comfort video generator** designed to bring peace, warmth, and spiritual comfort to your heart through personalized Bible reading videos. At Object2Workout, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using Object2Workout:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, provide customer support, and personalize your comfort video experience.

2. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use Object2Workout, including videos created, emotions selected, Bible verses chosen, and interaction patterns.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our AI video generation services, and provide more relevant comfort content.

3. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access Object2Workout, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility for video playback.

4. **Cookies**
   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences, maintain your session, and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services, remember your preferences, and personalize your experience.

5. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for video generation credits and subscriptions.

6. **Video Content and Images**
   - **What We Collect**: Images you upload for video generation and the videos you create through our service.
   - **Purpose**: To generate personalized Bible comfort videos according to your requests. We respect your privacy and handle this content with care.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Opt-out of certain data collection practices
- Export your video content
